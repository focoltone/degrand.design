Custom Reporting

Intro

The client is a digital marketing firm in the automotive industry serving several hundred dealerships in the southern United States. Our team needed to bring monthly data the client reported to their customers into a consolidated reporting tool.

Work Covered & Teams

April 1st - August 31st, 2018 | 10 Sprints

This project example covers one of the three custom dashboards we built for the client.This is the second in the series of three.

Our team: Business Analyst, QA, UI/UX Designer, Sr. Engineer, 3x Jr. Engineers, Sr. Architect.

Client team: VP of Digital Strategy and Operations, Lead Account Manager, Lead Specialist.

Design Tools

Sketch, Abstract, Zeplin, Invision, Jira, and Optimalworkshop for interviewing & research.

Client Background

Served the digital marketing needs of their clients through 90+ employees across nationwide offices for 40 years.

Problem

Our client spends many hours and resources preparing their monthly reports for their customers. They pull marketing data from multiple sources to create a monthly performance summary, which was time consuming and fragmented.

Challenge

Our team's challenge was to unify their data and allow managers to automate monthly performance summaries for their customers by creating a custom reporting dashboard that integrated with Twitter, Google, and Facebook.

Goals

Reduce the number of hours spent preparing monthly and one-off reports for customers.

Research

I advocated for time with employees who were already using the existing dashboard and those who will use the new one. We reported back on the information we collected, pointing out feedback that was insightful, giving the whole team a way to make informed decisions about the interface and data visualizations for the dashboard.

Interviews

In our initial interviews we asked targeted questions that uncovered how the client team members worked with their customers. We also learned a lot about what account managers and specialists were expecting to be able to accomplish with their customers using the dashboard.

I built a strong rapport with our client’s team members; some of them thanked me for taking the time to speak with them and wanted to be involved as much as possible going forward. I synthesized all of the feedback from the interviews, discovered themes, and reported them back to the client during our weekly calls. Our client quickly began to see the value in the interviews; they felt it was “easier to make decisions when we know how our people think and feel”.

Design

We worked with developers to determine which technologies for creating rich data visuals met our clients needs. We used a combination of libraries and custom built graphs to meet the demands. We chose each way of displaying the data based on our clients preference and their customers familiarity. Providing feedback from testing different options for charts and graphs that helped our client incorporate new ways of displaying their data.
